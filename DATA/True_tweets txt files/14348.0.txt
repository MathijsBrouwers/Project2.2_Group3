Residents on the Greek island of Lesbos went on strike on Monday to protest against European policies they say have turned it into a  prison  for migrants and refugees. Islanders shut businesses, shops, municipal offices, nurseries and pharmacies and dozens rallied on a central square, calling on the government to transfer asylum-seekers to the mainland.
