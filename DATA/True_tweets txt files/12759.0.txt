The future free trade agreement between the European Union and Britain will have to be along the same lines as the one the EU has with Canada, the EU s chief Brexit negotiation Michel Barnier said on Friday. Barnier said that there was no other option, given Britain s terms for a future relationship with the EU, after it leaves the bloc in 2019.
