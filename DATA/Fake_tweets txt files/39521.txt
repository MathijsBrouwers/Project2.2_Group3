If previously the Kingdom of the Netherlands used to punish for public expression of homophobia, according to the law adopted this summer, public statements, well-grounded doubts about intolerance towards LGBT community will become the reason for imposing fines and what is most important, deprivation of parental rights. Police have already reported that eight children were “taken away” from families on the very first day of enactment of the law. After three months of raising in special camps, children will be adopted by other families. Dutch Ombudsman for Children, Aster Maria Jacobs said that the State is doing its utmost to help new generation avoid “a nightmare of intolerance.
