2000 Years Old Mummy's Face Reconstruction Reveals a Beauty # 10 
Mummies are not always ugly and scary. 
Meritamun, as she has been named, was an ancient Egyptian woman whose 2000 years-old mummified body had been kept for over a century in a basement until researchers of University of Melbourne decided to proceed to a face reconstruction fearing of decaying. 
The process of face reconstruction, based on 3D scanning of the skull and population data of that time, revealed an ancient Egyptian beauty. Tags
