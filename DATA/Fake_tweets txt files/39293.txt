The Armed Forces of Ukraine opened fire on the civilian population of the Donetsk and Luhansk regions of the country. According to the representative of the operational command of the self-proclaimed "Donetsk People's Republic", Daniil Bezsonov, the Ukrainian military "fired targeted civilian vehicles with mortars of 82 mm caliber." The cars were parked near the Vasilyevskaya water pumping station in the Donetsk region. Mr. Bezsonov assured the incident occurred on January 17 and the Ukrainian shelling was performed with the assistance of a drone
